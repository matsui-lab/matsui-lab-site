Biomedical & Health Informatics, Nagoya University

Our Mission

The Matsui Lab is a data-driven, interdisciplinary research group at Nagoya University dedicated to advancing the fields of Biomedical and Health Informatics. We integrate cutting-edge computational biology and digital health to address complex challenges and shape the future of medicine.
